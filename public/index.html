PLAYER GUIDE
Everything you need to know · Zulu's Game Tracker v1.5
🗺️
LIVE MAP
Your GPS position and all teammates appear in real-time on the tactical map. Red markers are Red Team, blue markers are Blue Team. Your position updates every few seconds.
⚡
TACTICAL PERKS
Unlock powerful abilities during the game. UAV reveals enemy positions for 30s. EMP scrambles enemy GPS. Smoke hides your position. Airstrike marks targets. All have cooldowns.
🩺
MEDIC SYSTEM
When hit, press 🚨 MEDIC on the map screen. A Medic teammate can see your bleed-out timer and revive you before time runs out. Walk to a blue spawn zone when fully dead to respawn.
📡
COMMS
The Comms tab shows all orders from your Commander and Support role players. Commanders speak first — their orders appear at the top. Check comms regularly during play.
🔋
GPS + BATTERY
For best GPS on Android: Settings → Apps → Swindon Airsoft → Battery → Unrestricted. On iPhone, use Safari and keep your screen on with Field Mode. GPS accuracy is typically 3–8 metres.
🎯
OBJECTIVES
Admin sets objectives before each game — shown on your map and in the Comms tab. Capture zones by having your team inside them for the required time. Completed objectives turn green.
◈ TACTICAL PERKS GUIDE

📡
GPS FIELD MODE
To keep your GPS active during gameplay, your screen needs to stay on. When your screen locks, the browser suspends all JS including GPS — this is an OS-level restriction with no workaround.
Field Mode keeps your screen on and shows a minimal tactical display — like a satnav — so the screen-on makes sense and your battery is used wisely.
⚠ Your browser doesn't support automatic screen keep-on.

To keep GPS active you must prevent screen lock manually:
Android: Settings → Display → Screen timeout → set to max
Samsung: Settings → Display → Screen timeout → 10 minutes
iPhone: Settings → Display & Brightness → Auto-Lock → Never

Install as a home screen app (Add to Home Screen in your browser) for best results.

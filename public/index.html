PLAYER GUIDE
Everything you need to know · Zulu's Game Tracker v1.5
📱 Getting Started
Open the app, tap PLAY, select your map, enter a callsign, choose role and team, then DEPLOY. Grant location permission when asked — GPS is required for tracking. Register an account to save your callsign and session across refreshes.
🗺️ The Map Screen
Your position shows as a ★ star. Teammates show their role insignia. Enemy positions are always hidden unless revealed by UAV or Intel Hack. Coloured zones are drawn by your admin — tap for labels.
🚨 I Am Down — Medic Needed
Tap the big red button on the Status tab. A 2-minute bleed-out timer starts. Your 🆘 marker flashes on all teammates maps. You can be revived by: a Medic role player within 5m, the Medkit perk within 10m, or the timer expires and you must walk into a Respawn Zone. Once the timer expires, even a medic cannot revive you — respawn zone only.
📱 Keeping GPS Active
The app uses three layers to keep GPS alive when your screen locks: (1) a Screen Wake Lock to keep the display on, (2) a silent audio session that prevents the browser suspending the page (most reliable method), and (3) a Service Worker that caches your last position. On iOS: tap Share → Add to Home Screen, then open from there — Safari PWA mode gives the best background GPS. On Android: allow background activity for Chrome. Keep the app open in the foreground during gameplay for best results.
⚡ TACTICAL PERKS
📡
UAV SCAN 60s team
Scans 30m radius around you. Reveals nearby enemies to your team for 20s. Enemy team gets a warning but no positions.
⚡
EMP BLAST 4m30s team
Scrambles enemy GPS within 100m for 30s. Their positions show incorrectly — random jitter on your map.
💨
SMOKE SCREEN 3min team
Drops smoke at your position for 30s. Hides your GPS from enemies while active. Visible to all as a grey zone.
💻
INTEL HACK 2m30s personal
Reveals one random enemy's position to you only for 10s. Personal cooldown — your teammates can still hack.
🩹
MEDKIT 2min team
Instantly revives all downed teammates within 10m. Only works before the bleed-out timer expires.
🚁
AIR SUPPORT 4min team
Calls suppression fire at your position. Marks a 10m hazard zone for 60s. Alerts all players including enemies.
🪖 Roles & Insignia
⚔ Assault — Two chevrons (British infantry stripes). 🎯 Sniper — Star with scope (marksman badge). 🛡 Support — Crown (British Army support symbol). ✚ Medic — Geneva cross in circle (NATO/British medic). 👁 Scout — Binoculars (recce). ★ Commander — Crown and orb (officer rank). Role types and max numbers are set per map by your admin.
❓ Common Issues
Map won't show position: Check browser location permission. Move outdoors for GPS signal.
Perk greyed out: Either you're bleeding out, or your team's cooldown is active.
Can't see teammates: They may not have GPS yet — check Squad tab.
Disconnected: The app auto-reconnects. Refresh if needed — session is saved.
Role says "full": Admin has set a max for that role — choose another.
ABOUT ZGT
Zulu's Game Tracker · Built for Airsoft
What is Zulu's Game Tracker?
A real-time tactical GPS tracking app built specifically for airsoft games. Players join a game map on their phone, and their positions are shared with their team in real time on an interactive Leaflet map. Enemy positions stay hidden until revealed through tactical perks.
Built by
Chris Zulu · Swindon Airsoft
Version 1.1.0 · Built with Node.js, WebSockets, Leaflet.js and PostgreSQL.
Features
Live GPS tracking · Red vs Blue teams · 6 tactical perks · Bleed-out system · Admin zone drawing · Saved map templates · Multi-admin management · Player accounts · Role limits per map · Military insignia · Background GPS keepalive · PostgreSQL persistent storage
Admin Panel
Game administrators access the panel at /admin. The super admin can create maps, draw zones, manage site admins, view player accounts, use tactical perks, and control all aspects of the game.

KEEP SCREEN ON?
During a game your phone screen may lock, stopping GPS and disconnecting you from the battlefield.
📱 Manual fix: Go to Settings → Display → Screen timeout and set it to "Never" or the longest option. On Samsung: Settings → Display → Screen timeout. On iPhone: Settings → Display & Brightness → Auto-Lock → Never.
